We have a lot of excited things planned for the kids and youth during Camp Meeting 2020. For more information about Springfield Camp Meeting, or to register, see below.

About

The Central District Church of God Camp Meeting is a week long gathering of all members of the Church of God in the central district of Ohio.

Youth

During the week, the youth (students entering grades 7-12) will have morning devotionals, afternoon activities, a nightly service, and late night activities. For a full schedule, see below. Youth who volunteer and are chosen to work in the dining hall will receive free food and lodging for the week.


Children

There will be morning activities for the children, an afternoon activity, and a class during the service. There will also be a nursery available during all services for babies/infants.
